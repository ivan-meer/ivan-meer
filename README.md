## Engineering Philosophy

I believe the future of software engineering is shifting from writing every line of code manually to designing reliable systems where humans and AI collaborate effectively.

My focus is on building reproducible workflows, robust architectures, and reusable engineering patterns that make AI-powered development faster, safer, and easier to scale.
